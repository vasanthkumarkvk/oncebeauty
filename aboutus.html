About Anusha



Anusha manages the One Stop Beauty salon with 10 female employees, building the business from scratch to fulfil her childhood dream. Anusha has been in the finance industry for over 25 years and now has expanded to the beauty industry as she has always wanted to start a 'Women Working With Women' business to support and empower women, particularly in the beauty industry.

She is a strong woman that sets an example to many other women, with her motto being that Passions can also become reality if you BELIEVE in yourself.

Women are not given opportunities or provided enough support to make dreams come true. Anusha has 10 female employees, taking the first step of many to help them make their dreams come true. One Stop Beauty has female employees from many different cultures, race and age groups.

Her staff are multi-skilled and are qualified industry-recognised and now part of the biggest and the best multi-service beauty salon in Western Sydney.

One Stop Beauty Salon was built for Women by Women and our vision is to be the Biggest and Best Beauty service salon in Australia!
